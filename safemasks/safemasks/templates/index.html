{% trans 'How does our rating work?' %}

{% blocktrans %} We review both, the supplier and products provided by the supplier, on a 3-scale basis—positive (+1), neutral (0) and negative (-1)—utilizing different sources. This allows as to formulate aggreate ratings from different sources based on

  • the number (how many source do we have?),
  • date (when was the last update?),
  • accuracy (how detailed was the report?) and
  • outcome (was the review positive?)

A supplier is considered trustworthy if the average rating is above 0.5.

{% endblocktrans %}
